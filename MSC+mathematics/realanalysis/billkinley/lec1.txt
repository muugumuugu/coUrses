===== Completness Axiom =====
* real numbers satisfy Field Axioms, Order Axioms,and in adition the LUB axiom:
	* for any non-empty above(below)-bounded set in ℝ, there exists a supremum(infimum) in ℝ 
* gives rise directly to
	* **archimedean property**
	* **well-ordering principle**
