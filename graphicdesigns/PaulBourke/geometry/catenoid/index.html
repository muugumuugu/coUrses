Catenoid Minimal Surface

Written by Paul Bourke
December 2000
x = c cosh(v/c) cos(u)
y = c cosh(v/c) sin(u)
z = v
0 <= u <= 2pi
-infinity <= v <= infinity


The Catenoid is the only minimal surface (zero mean curvature) that is also a surface of revolution. It was first documented by Leonhard Euler around 1740 making it the oldest documented minimal surface.
