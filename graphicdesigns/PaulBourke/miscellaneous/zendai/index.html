Exhibition: Deep Space
Zendai Museum of Modern Art, Shanghai

Hellen Sky (Company in Space), Paul Bourke
Sound score: David Franzke
Exhibition brochure
July-September 2006


"Deep Space" by Hellen Sky as part of the "Strange attractors" exhibition at the Zendai Museum of Modern Art, Shanghai from late July until the end of August. Strange Attractors explores connections between art and science, featuring works resulting from collaborations between scientists and artists.

 
Courtesy Tim Bateson

Photos by Hellen Sky

Technical

Original plan, projection onto three walls and the floor.

4 projectors (A,B,C,D) are driven from one Mac G5 with two dualhead2go Matrox boxes. The imagery/audio is driven by two Quartz Composer scripts.
