Topic: Dome projection on a budget (Mirrordome)

APS (Australasian Planetarium Society), Auckland, August 2005

Presented by Paul Bourke
August 2005


APS 2005 conference was held at the Auckland planetarium (StarDome) located in the One Tree Hill Cornwell park. The slides here are a presentation given on a low cost projection solution, the entire presentation was performed using the proposed spherical mirror projection inside the 11m dome.
