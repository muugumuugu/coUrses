Portfolio: 3D reconstruction from photographs (photogrammetry)

I offer an end-to-end photographic 3D reconstruction service, from taking the photographs, textured mesh reconstruction to mesh editing and cleanup (if necessary). A small but representative selection of past projects can be found below. Pricing to reconstruct objects of your choosing depends on their nature and is quoted on a case by case basis. For more information please contact the author by email, preferably with a few photographs of the objects in question. Other examples can be found on my SketchFab channel.
