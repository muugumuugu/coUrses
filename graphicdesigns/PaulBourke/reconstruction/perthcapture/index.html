Capturing Perth

Paul Bourke
Collated mostly in 2020


The first are a few sample examples from a contract to capture various 3D assets from public places in Perth. A preference was given to 3D assets, whether derived from 3D reconstruction or semi-manual modelling. Following that is a collection of street art, both smaller scale and panoramic. In each case clicking on the preview image will present a higher resolution version.
