iDome and digital projection into hemispherical domes

Presentation to the Astronomical Group of Western Australia

Paul Bourke


Abstract

I will discuss a new innovation developed by myself for projecting into hemispherical domes. This will include a discussion of immersive environments, their applications, how one might go about creating content (both computer generated and live footage), and a general survey of the planetarium industry. This will be a very informal discussion, questions are most welcome at any stage.
