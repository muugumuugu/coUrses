Vertical dome (iDome):
Visualisation and Navigable Movies

APS (Australasian Planetarium Society)

Horizon - The Planetarium, Perth

Presented by Paul Bourke
May 2008


Abstract

This talk will present a number of activities revolving around the iDome. This will include examples of work by collaborators, description and examples of the warp movie player and Quartz Composer Patch, examples of image and movie capture, and finally some of the future work being considered.
