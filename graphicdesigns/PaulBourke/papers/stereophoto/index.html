Stereoscopic Photography

P.D. Bourke
Workshop presented at University of Western Australia, September 2007


The workshop will cover capturing simple stereo-pairs as well as images suitable for use with Sirovision. The basic principles relating to projection will be introduced and how they apply to stereoscopic photography. Particular emphasis will be give to how one creates good quality stereoscopic image pairs that place minimal stress on the visual system.

Attendees are encouraged to bring their own laptops if they want to experiment taking stereo photos, editing, and displaying the result on the projection system provided.
