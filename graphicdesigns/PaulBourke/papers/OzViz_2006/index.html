OzViz 2006 was hosted by the Western Australian Supercomputer Project (WASP) a new visualisation facility at the University of Western Australia, in association with iVEC and the UWA Communication Studies Program. Ozviz2006 was generously supported by the Australian Partnership for Advanced Computing (APAC) and iVEC. The workshop was be held in the spirit of previous ones: short, practical presentations by people who are active practitioners in the visualisation field. OzViz ran between October Mon 30 - Tues 31 2006. Principal Organisers were: Paul Bourke (WASP Vizlab), Karen Haines (Director, WASP), Peter Morse (UWA Communication Studies), Drew Whitehouse (ANUSF Vizlab).
