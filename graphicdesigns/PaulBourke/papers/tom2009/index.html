Novel presentation of visualisation results using
rapid prototypes, crystal engraving,
holograms and the iDome.

Written by Paul Bourke

Tomography Workshop: "Scientific Application of X-ray Tomography in Perth"
UWA, June 2009


Researchers in this room most commonly generate so called volumetric datasets. These need to be visualised (preferably in real-time) and often the visualisation integrated with derived statistics and analysis. In this talk I will present some of the more unusual visualisation options being explored at UWA, these include rapid prototyping, crystal engraving, holograms, and the immersive iDome.

At the end of the meeting attendees are invited to visit the WASP for a first hand demonstration of the techniques discussed.
