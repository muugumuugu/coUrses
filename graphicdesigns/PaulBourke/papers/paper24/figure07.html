Simulation of local field potential time series, power spectral content, and cross-corrlations of two sites in the cortical receptive field, stimulated by combinations of moving bars in the visual field. Input signals within a bar are spatially coherent, while the signals input to separate bars are uncorrelated. Signal/noise ratio (point amplitude of bar driving signals /point amplitude of incoherent background noise) = 300.
