Engaging displays for Visualisation

Future Science Conference

STAWA: Science Teachers Association of Western Australia

Presented by Paul Bourke
Perth, November 2011


Description

This workshop will present novel and engaging ways of visualising data. A key theme will be how to fully exploit the capabilities of the human visual system above what is normally experienced on a flat 2D computer display.

Workshop will be held in the Vislab, iVEC@UWA, ground floor Physics Building.
