Virtual Environments, Visualisation, Novel Displays

Workshop: 3D visualisation: Evolution of thought. Perth, April 2011.
Presented by Paul Bourke
April 2011


Paul will present on a number of projects over the last few years around virtual heritage. That is, using virtual environments to present an alternative sense of place, time, and culture. An emphasis will be placed on novel capture and display technologies including multiple stereoscopic walls, high resolution displays, and cylindrical/spherical immersive displays.
