APAC 2007 Visualisation Showreel

8 Oct 2007 - 10 Oct 2007
Perth, Western Australia

Conveners: Paul Bourke (UWA), Drew Whitehouse (ANU)

At APAC 2007 a digital showreel of visualisation content will be shown during the conference on a Sony 4K projector. The showreel consists of short animations from those involved in scientific visualisation within Australian research institutions.
