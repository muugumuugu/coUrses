Data capture for VR

Presented by Paul Bourke
VRLab@ECU, June 2017


At the meeting this Friday I will briefly present two types of data capture I have been performing in the last month, both designed for presentation in VR environments. The first is the capture of 3D objects, specifically, sacred Buddhist relics from Sri Lanka (and earlier Cambodia). The second is an ultra high resolution stereoscopic panorama stills camera, I'll bring the locally developed camera rig along (you won't see it anywhere else).
