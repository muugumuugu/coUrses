Advances in Visualisation

iVEC eResearch Forum
February 2008
P.D. Bourke


This presentation considers the future of commodity based visualisation techniques and hardware. In particular the suggestion that advances in larger industries can be leveraged to solve visualisation problems at a lower price point thus making them accessible to a wider research community. Three particular areas will be discussed: gaming technologies for collaborative visualisation, HiDef television and the advances in monitor and projection displays, the rapid raise in graphics card performance and techniques that can achieve higher visual quality.
