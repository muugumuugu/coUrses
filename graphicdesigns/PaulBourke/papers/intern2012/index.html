The Diversity of Visualisation:
Selected visualisation projects from 2011

Presented by Paul Bourke
February 2012


Abstract

Visualisation activities at iVEC@UWA during 2011 will be presented. This will include projects from very diverse fields such as the visualisation of large scale simulation data in astrophysics research, video capture for immersive virtual heritage experiences, and visualisations from a new random space filling algorithm in 1, 2, and 3 dimensions.
