Images from a temporary Museum exhibit called

To Mars and Beyond

A 10 minute stereoscopic 3D animation of a flight that leaves Earth, flies around Mars, and finally lands on Mars. Prepared for the National Museum of Australia (December 2001) by Astrophysics and Supercomputing, Swinburne University.
