Stereographics, 3D projection

Written by Paul Bourke

The following contains information around stereographics, it includes theory and practical installation matters (both hardware and software). Examples from the authors practice can be found in this photographic gallery of stereoscopic installations , mostly (but not entirely) passive (polaroid based) stereoscopic projection systems.

Please note that I am available as a consultant on all matters relating to the material found here.
