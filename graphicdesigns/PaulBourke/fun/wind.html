Wind and sound

Why, when you shout against the wind, is it harder to make yourself heard compared to shouting with the wind?

The speed of sound in air is around 330m/s while a reasonably strong wind might be only 10m/s, in other words the speed of wind air is insignificant compared to the speed of sound.
