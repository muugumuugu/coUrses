Explorer

An explorer needs to cross a desert that takes 6 days on foot. He/she will need to hire bearers to take food because any one person can only carry enough food and water for 4 days. What is the smallest number of bearers needed?
ps: It isn't acceptable to leave the bearers in the desert to die.
