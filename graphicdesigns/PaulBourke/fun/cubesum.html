Sum of Cubes

What is the smallest number (besides 0 and 1) where the sum of the cube power of the digits is equal to the number itself?

For example: if a 4 digit number is abcd then what numbers have the property that

abcd = a*a*a + b*b*b + d*d*d + c*c*c
