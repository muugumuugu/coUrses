Boolardy photography

The following are various digital assets captured by Paul Bourke and Jonathan Knispel from the ASKAP (Australian Square Kilometer Array) site on the 3 June 2010. It includes photographs of the current dish, the wide field array, time lapse of the dish in motion, high resolution panorama imagery, and LadyBug-3 360x150 degree video.
