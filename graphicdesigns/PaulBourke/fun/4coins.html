Rearranging 4 coins

Contributed by Daniel Eriksson

Rearrange the coins in the formation on the left to the formation on the right. The rules for moving coins are as follows:
- Only one coin can be moved at a time
- When a coin is moved it must be placed so that it always touches 2 coins.
- Coins may not be lifted off the surface
