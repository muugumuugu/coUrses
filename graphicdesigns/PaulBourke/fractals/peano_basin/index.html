Peano Basin

Written by Paul Bourke
January 2017


The Peano Basin fractal documented in the 1890s as one of a series of curves described by Giuseppe Peano. It is space filling with fractal dimension of 2.

The Peano basin has been proposed as a mathematical model for river basin networks.
