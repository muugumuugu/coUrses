Drift Prints

Recently I made three (identical) very high quality prints on aquarelle paper. The motif is generated using the snek framework, and the particular algorithm is somewhat arbitrarily named "Drift".

The prints are 40x40 cm (+ ~4 cm padding), and they are printed on heavy (fine) aquarelle paper. Finally, the prints have been varnished. If handeled correctly, the prints should last for a very long time. As you can see in the picture below the prints are signed and numbered from 1 to 3.

All prints have been sold.

The prints are 600 USD each. This includes postage to anywhere in the world. Note that I always mark the packages as sales, so you may have to pay taxes/VAT depending on your local rules.
