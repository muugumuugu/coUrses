Mechanical Plotter Drawings (2)

After digging through some of my plotter drawings I decided to make three more available to anyone who is interested.

These drawings were made in 2016 on my Makeblock plotter. They are drawn on sheets of acid-free paper using pigmented ink. All three plots are ~40 x 40 cm, in addition they have several cm of padding on all sides. Since they have been plotted using a mechanical plotter they all have various kinds of artefacts, as you can see in the close ups below.

The plots are 300 USD each. This includes postage to anywhere in the world. Note that I always mark the packages as sales, so you may have to pay taxes/VAT depending on your local rules.

If you want to buy one, or if you have questions, please contact me by e-mail. Make sure to include the name of the drawing you are interested in. Eg. 4ec36f5.

If you want to buy a plot make sure to also include your full address. I will then send you the payment details (Paypal). Note that I will not ship the plot until I have received they payment in full.
