Mechanical Plotter Drawings (3)

Here are three ~21 x 21 cm plots using white ink on dark paper. The plots were drawn with the Axidraw plotter using white Sakura Gelly Rolls on smooth 200 g Fabriano Colore paper . The plots are uncut, so there are several centimeters of padding on all sides.

Note that there are two kinds of paper used in the plots below, Nero is black, while Ferro is a medium gray. You should also be aware that the gelly roll ink is not really absorbed into the paper. Because of this it adds a bit of texture. This also means that there is some flaking and clumping in areas with a lot of ink. These details are interesting in my opinion, but you may not agree with me. You can see this to some extent in the photographs, but it is a little difficult to photograph it properly.

The plots are 300 USD each. This includes postage to anywhere in the world. I always mark the packages as sales, so you may have to pay taxes/VAT depending on your local rules.
