Mechanical Plotter Drawings

Over the past few weeks I have made several generative plotter drawings using my Axidraw mechanical plotter. The drawings are generated using (parts of) my new experimental system written in Common Lisp. You can read more about that here. I've decided to make a few of them available for anyone interested.

The plots are 300 USD each. This includes postage to anywhere in the world. Note that I always mark the packages as sales, so you may have to pay taxes/VAT depending on your local rules.

All drawings are unique. They are drawn using pigmented ink (Micron pens) on high quality (A3) sheets of acid-free paper. Drawings marked with Bristol 250 are drawn on Fabriano Bristol 250 g/m2, and those marked with t6·240 are drawn on Fabriano Tecnico 6 240 g/m2. Since they have been plotted using a mechanical plotter they all have their own little oddities and artefacts.

If you want to buy one, or if you have questions, please contact me by e-mail. Make sure to include the name of the drawing you are interested in. Eg. 8185bea.

If you want to buy a plot make sure to also include your full address. I will then send you the payment details (Paypal). Note that I will not ship the plot until I have received they payment in full.

As you can see in the photos, the plots are marked with their name and date of creation. Before I ship them I will sign and number (1/1) them inside the plotted area of ~ 21 x 21 cm. That way they can be cropped into a square before framing. I ship them without cropping because this makes them easier to handle.
